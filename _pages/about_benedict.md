Dr. Benedict Jun MA
---
Assistant Professor

PhD in Industrial Engineering, University of Hong Kong, 2024

Dr. Benedict Jun Ma is a tenure-track Assistant Professor in the Intelligent Transportation Thrust at The Hong Kong University of Science and Technology (Guangzhou), where he is leading the Intelligent Logistics and Supply Chain Analytics (iLSCA) Lab. Prior to this, he was a Postdoctoral Associate at Massachusetts Institute of Technology, affiliated with the MIT Center for Transportation and Logistics (CTL) and the MIT Digital Supply Chain Lab. He received his PhD from the Department of Data and Systems Engineering at The University of Hong Kong. He was also a visiting researcher at Mines Paris – PSL University, Erasmus University Rotterdam, and NHH. 

For more information, please visit his [faculty website](https://facultyprofiles.hkust-gz.edu.cn/faculty-personal-page/MA-BenedictJun/junm) or [personal website](https://benedictjunma.github.io/).
