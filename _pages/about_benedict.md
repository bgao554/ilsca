Dr. Benedict Jun MA
---
Assistant Professor

PhD in Industrial Engineering, University of Hong Kong, 2024

Dr. Benedict Jun Ma is a tenure-track Assistant Professor in the Intelligent Transportation Thrust at The Hong Kong University of Science and Technology (Guangzhou), where he is leading the Intelligent Logistics and Supply Chain Analytics (iLSCA) Lab. Prior to this, he was a Postdoctoral Associate at Massachusetts Institute of Technology, affiliated with the MIT Center for Transportation and Logistics (CTL) and the MIT Digital Supply Chain Lab. He received his PhD from the Department of Data and Systems Engineering at The University of Hong Kong. He was also a visiting researcher at Mines Paris – PSL University, Erasmus University Rotterdam, and NHH. 

His research explores how emerging technologies enhance the modeling, optimization, and control of systems in transportation, logistics, and supply chains. He has collaborated closely with leading global companies (e.g., Maersk, Johnson&Johnson, and GE Vernova). His works have been published in top journals such as MIT Sloan Management Review (FT50), IEEE Transactions on Engineering Management, Computer-Aided Civil and Infrastructure Engineering, Journal of the Operational Research Society, International Journal of Production Economics, International Journal of Production Research, and Transportation Research Part D/Part E, as well as widely read practitioner outlets such as Supply Chain Management Review.
